Maarfapad manual

Note for early testers:

  1. Currently you're limited to making only 2 wikis under your account for technical reasons.
  2. Before saving, check your Maarfapad controls to make sure you're saving to the server.
  3. Due to differences in http protocol, the official plugin library will not work behind maarfapad though you can still install new plugins by dragging and dropping plugin tiddlers into your wiki. Another workaround is to download your wiki onto your machine. While it is outside maarfapad, you can then use the official plugin library, install new plugins, upload it via Maarfapad controls and refresh.
  4. Feel free to submit feedback either to @maarfapad on twitter or use the Send Feedback tiddler in your wiki.
  5. Try everything out, make it your own. If anything seems faulty in any way, please say so. It'll be appreciated.
  6. Always back up your data. You can always download your stuff via Maarfapad controls
  7. Last of all, be careful when you edit data on two devices simultaneously. You might overwrite your previous data if you're not careful. This will be taken care of soon so bear with me. It's a one man show currently :-)
